# Profile Photo Setup

## Required File

Add your professional photo to this directory with the following specifications:

**File Name:** `yishnu-profile.jpg`

**Photo Requirements:**

### Content Guidelines
- **Professional headshot** - shoulders up, centered
- **Background** - Clean, neutral background (solid color preferred)
- **Expression** - Approachable, confident smile
- **Attire** - Professional business casual
- **Lighting** - Well-lit, no harsh shadows
- **Eye contact** - Looking directly at camera

### Technical Specifications
- **Format:** JPG or PNG
- **Minimum Resolution:** 800x800 pixels (square)
- **Recommended Resolution:** 1200x1200 pixels
- **Aspect Ratio:** 1:1 (square)
- **File Size:** Under 500KB for optimal performance
- **Color Space:** sRGB

### Image Processing
The photo will be automatically:
- Cropped to perfect circle
- Displayed at 300px (desktop), 200px (tablet), 150px (mobile)
- Bordered with purple-to-green gradient
- Enhanced with subtle glow effect
- Optimized for lazy loading

### Placement
Your photo appears in two locations:
1. **Hero Section** - Top right corner (primary placement with animations)
2. **Footer** - Left side next to copyright (static, 80px)

## Quick Setup

1. Take or obtain a professional headshot photo
2. Crop to square (1:1 aspect ratio)
3. Export as JPG at 1200x1200 pixels
4. Optimize file size to under 500KB
5. Rename to `yishnu-profile.jpg`
6. Place in this directory (`public/images/`)
7. Refresh your website

The photo will appear immediately on both Hero and Footer sections with all animations and styling applied automatically.
